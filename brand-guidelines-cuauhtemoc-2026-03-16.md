## Brand Story

### Mission Statement
To empower business owners by building high-performance digital presences, bridging the gap between elite engineering and strategic marketing "a la carte".

### Vision Statement
To be the "Aston Martin" of digital consulting: where robust technical reliability meets sophisticated brand strategy and holistic design.

### Core Values
- **Technical Integrity**: Bulletproof code (Java/Python) as the foundation of every project.
- **Marketing Literacy**: Analysis rooted in branding, communication, and digital presence.
- **Holistic Strategy**: Seeing the "full forest" (business goals) before the "trees" (code).
- **Elite Reliability**: An "Aston Martin" experience—premium, robust, and dependable.

### Brand Personality
**The Aston Martin**: Sophisticated, powerful, reliable, and high-tech. Authoritative yet distinguished.

### Brand Archetype
**Primary**: **The Magician** (Transforming business frustration into digital authority)  
**Secondary**: **The Ruler** (Commanding technical and strategic excellence)

## Visual Identity

### Logo
Currently represented by the high-contrast wordmark "Cuauhtémoc Cataño" in **Archivo Black**.

**Usage Guidelines**:
- Clear space: 1x height of the 'C'
- Minimum size: 120px weight for web
- Font: Archivo, Bold (700)

### Color Palette (Liquid Glass System)

#### Primary Colors (Dark Mode)
- **OLED Black**: `#09090B` (Background)
- **Electric Blue**: `#2563EB` (Accent/Buttons/Icons)
- **Pure White**: `#FAFAFA` (Text/Main Titles)

#### Primary Colors (Light Mode)
- **Paper White**: `#FAFAFA` (Background)
- **Deep Slate**: `#09090B` (Text/Main Titles)
- **Electric Blue**: `#2563EB` (Accent)

#### Glass Effects
- **Blur**: 12px saturate(180%)
- **Border**: White/10% (Dark), Black/5% (Light)

#### Accessibility
- **Contrast Ratio**: 4.5:1 minimum for all functional text.
- **Focus States**: 2px solid Electric Blue.-

### Typography

#### Primary Typeface: Archivo
**Weights**: Bold (700), SemiBold (600)  
**Usage**: Headers, Page Titles, Navigation

#### Secondary Typeface: Space Grotesk
**Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500)  
**Usage**: Body text, Subtitles, Technical descriptions

## Voice and Messaging

### Brand Voice
Authoritative, Technical, Conscious, and Direct.

**Key Characteristics**:
1. **The Architect**: Uses precise terms, speaks in systems and structures.
2. **The Mentor**: Explains the "Why" behind the "How", encourages growth.
3. **The Yogui**: Grounded, calm, avoids "hacker" clichés, focuses on balance.

### Tone Variations

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Technical Docs | Precise | "Implementing a thread-safe singleton pattern in Java." |
| Podcast | Conversational | "How do we transition from being the 'doer' to the 'owner'?" |
| About Me | Personal | "Base in Mexico City, creating a bridge between code and strategy." |

### Digital

#### Website (cuauhtemoccatano.github.io)
The primary portfolio should use the "Liquid Glass" style exclusively.

#### Podcast (Spotify/YouTube/Apple)

## Usage Examples

### Good Examples
- High-contrast BW photography for Dark Mode.
- Clean, masonry grids for project display.
- Terminal-inspired UI elements for technical authority.

### Bad Examples
- Using generic emojis like 🚀 💻 (Use Lucide/BoxIcons instead).
- Low contrast text on glass backgrounds.
- Shaky or pixelated image assets.
